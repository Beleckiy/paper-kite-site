Paper Kite Holding is a multi-entity manufacturing group covering the full value chain of paper-based packaging — from corrugated board and cartons to labels, printed materials, and book production.

Legacy

22 years of operation have produced real assets

Paper Kite Holding holds an established client base, production-grade printing and processing equipment, a proven product and service range, experienced engineering and operations staff, a vetted supplier network, justified inventory positions, and a market reputation that required decades to build.

Leadership is committed to results, not maintenance. A market reputation that required decades to build - serving customers across Ukraine and recognized in international markets as a reliable production partner.

The growth agenda

New product segments are in development. Equipment investment continues. Personnel capability is being built. Digital printing is a strategic direction. Business digitalization and AI integration are in motion. Financial instruments are being deployed. The foundation is solid. The trajectory is forward.
